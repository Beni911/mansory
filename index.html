About MANSORY

MANSORY stands at the pinnacle of automotive refinement, combining cutting-edge design with unmatched craftsmanship. From exotic supercars to elite SUVs, every vehicle we touch becomes a bold statement of individuality and prestige.

With over 30 years of experience, our passion for perfection is reflected in every carbon fiber detail, bespoke interior, and power upgrade. MANSORY isn't just tuning – it's transformation.

Our History

1989 - Foundation

MANSORY is founded in Brand, Germany, specializing in high-end automotive customization.

1996 - First Major Project

Completion of our first full vehicle customization for a private client, establishing our reputation for excellence.

2005 - Carbon Fiber Innovation

Pioneering the use of forged carbon fiber in automotive design, revolutionizing the industry.

2012 - Global Expansion

Opening of international showrooms in Dubai, Moscow, and Monaco to serve our growing clientele.

2018 - Record-Breaking Models

Introduction of our Venatus line, setting new standards for performance and luxury in SUV customization.

2023 - Present Day

Continuing to push boundaries with cutting-edge designs and performance upgrades for the world's most exclusive vehicles.
